Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your email address and password (hashed for security)
  • Expense Data: Transaction details you enter, including amount, category, date, and description
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you interact with the application

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your personal expense tracking dashboard
  • To generate spending summaries and category breakdowns
  • To allow filtering and searching of your transactions
  • To maintain the security and integrity of your account

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in a local database. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms
  • Database access is restricted and authenticated
  • Regular backups are performed to prevent data loss
  • Your expense data is private and only accessible to you

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. We do not use external analytics or advertising services that track your activity.

The only external services we use are:

  • Google Fonts for typography (loads font files only)
  • Web hosting infrastructure (your data remains encrypted and isolated)

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your expense data anytime from your dashboard
  • Edit: Modify or delete any expense entry at any time
  • Delete: Close your account and permanently delete all your data

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@spendly.example.com

We take your privacy seriously and will respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
